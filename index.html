How It Works

From Registration to Victory

Five simple steps from sign-up to competing on the global stage.

1

Register Your Team

Form a team of up to 5 students using your university email. Join your professor's class tournament for free, or elect into the Global Championship.

2

Set Your Policy Levers

Each round, adjust 10 macroeconomic levers — interest rates, fiscal spending, trade policy, and more.

3

Respond to Crises

Random economic events hit every team. How you respond determines your resilience score.

4

Climb the Leaderboard

Scores update after each round. The top 8 teams enter the playoff bracket. Prize pool is hypothetical — final percentage at management discretion.

5

Win Real Prizes

Winner takes 70%, runner-up takes 30% of the prize pool. Top 8 teams qualify for the playoff bracket. Certificates issued to all participants.
